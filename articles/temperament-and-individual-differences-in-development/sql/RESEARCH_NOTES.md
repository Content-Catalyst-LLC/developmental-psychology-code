# Research Notes: sql

This subfolder is part of the research-grade companion directory for:

**Temperament and Individual Differences in Development**

## Function of this folder

The `sql/` folder contributes to a reproducible, multi-language research workflow for modeling temperament and individual differences: reactivity, inhibition, activity level, caregiver support, classroom fit, chronic stress, acute stress, accommodation, teacher responsiveness, movement flexibility, goodness of fit, and developmental adjustment over time.

## Article directory

`articles/temperament-and-individual-differences-in-development`

## Research commitments

- temperament as early individuality, not moral verdict;
- developmental outcome as temperament-context interaction;
- regulation as co-regulated and relational;
- goodness of fit as a core mechanism;
- cultural interpretation, inequality, neurodivergence, and institutional response as developmental factors;
- ethical caution against high-stakes prediction or deficit labeling;
- transparent notes and schemas.
