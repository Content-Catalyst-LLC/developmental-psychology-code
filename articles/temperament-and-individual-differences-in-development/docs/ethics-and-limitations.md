# Ethics and Limitations

## Not a clinical, school-discipline, diagnostic, or risk-prediction tool

This repository must not be used for diagnosis, disability screening, school discipline, special education eligibility, clinical triage, student tracking, surveillance, risk scoring, child-welfare decision-making, or any high-stakes decision.

## Key risks

- reducing children to temperament scores;
- treating reactivity, inhibition, activity, or sensitivity as moral failure;
- confusing classroom mismatch with pathology;
- ignoring race, class, disability, language, gender, culture, family stress, and school resources;
- mistaking synthetic output for empirical evidence;
- ranking children, classrooms, schools, caregivers, or communities;
- using goodness-of-fit language to avoid providing real support or accommodation.

## Responsible use

Use this repository to teach and explore:

- temperament as early individual difference;
- child-context interaction;
- co-regulation and caregiving;
- classroom fit and accommodation;
- support, stress, and developmental pathways;
- synthetic longitudinal modeling.

Responsible real-world work requires validated measures, developmental expertise, privacy protection, family and community context, careful causal design, institutional review, anti-bias safeguards, disability rights awareness, and attention to dignity.
