# Ethics and Limitations

## Not a screening, placement, ranking, diagnostic, trauma, or maturity tool

This repository must not be used for developmental screening, school placement, disability classification, trauma prediction, maturity scoring, clinical diagnosis, risk prediction, eligibility decisions, child ranking, student tracking, surveillance, or any high-stakes decision.

## Key risks

- treating continuity as destiny;
- treating discontinuity as failure;
- confusing institutional rupture with individual deficit;
- ranking children, adolescents, adults, cultures, disabled people, or neurodivergent people by narrow developmental expectations;
- ignoring context, language, disability, neurodivergence, schooling, poverty, trauma, migration, and institutional inequality;
- mistaking synthetic output for empirical evidence;
- using developmental-change language to deny opportunity, support, accommodation, or dignity.

## Responsible use

Use this repository to teach and explore:

- continuous growth and nonlinear growth;
- threshold and logistic transition models;
- context-sensitive developmental timing;
- intervention and rupture as turning points;
- the critique of simplistic continuity/discontinuity binaries;
- synthetic longitudinal modeling.

Responsible real-world research requires validated measures, longitudinal design, cultural humility, disability rights awareness, trauma-informed practice, careful causal inference, anti-bias safeguards, and attention to developmental dignity.
