# Research Notes: cpp

This subfolder is part of the research-grade companion directory for:

**Continuity, Discontinuity, and the Logic of Developmental Change**

## Function of this folder

The `cpp/` folder contributes to a reproducible, multi-language workflow for modeling developmental change: continuous growth, nonlinear growth, threshold-sensitive shifts, logistic transitions, contextual support, chronic stress, resource stability, intervention exposure, rupture exposure, transition readiness, and developmental trajectories over time.

## Article directory

`articles/continuity-discontinuity-and-the-logic-of-developmental-change`

## Research commitments

- continuity and discontinuity as complementary developmental lenses;
- gradual accumulation and threshold reorganization as different shapes of change;
- support, stress, institutions, trauma, intervention, culture, disability, and inequality as developmental conditions;
- ethical caution against ranking, screening, or universalizing narrow developmental pathways;
- transparent notes and schemas.
