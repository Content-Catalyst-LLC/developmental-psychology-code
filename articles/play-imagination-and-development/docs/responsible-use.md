# Responsible Use

## Scope

This repository provides transparent synthetic workflows for play-development trajectory modeling. It is best used for teaching, documentation, reproducible methods, and prototype analysis design.

## Data governance

Any empirical extension should include documented data provenance, privacy protections, age-appropriate research design, accessibility considerations, disability-rights review, cultural humility, anti-bias review, and clear separation between descriptive modeling and causal claims.

## Interpretation

Play data should be interpreted as contextual and developmental. Scores, profiles, and trajectories should be used to understand systems of play support, access, restriction, peer inclusion, and developmental opportunity rather than to reduce children to fixed categories of readiness, creativity, or competence.
