# Model Card

## Name

Synthetic play, imagination, and development trajectory model.

## Intended use

Research education, article companion materials, methods teaching, longitudinal analysis practice, play-development modeling, and reproducible workflow development.

## Inputs

Synthetic variables representing baseline development, pretend play, social play, constructive play, outdoor play, caregiver support, chronic stress, play-space quality, adult responsiveness, inclusion climate, outdoor safety, play material access, current stress, play restriction, peer inclusion, play support context, and developmental outcome.

## Outputs

Longitudinal synthetic panel, profile summaries, context summaries, dynamic regression outputs, trajectory plots, scenario comparisons, and validation summaries.

## Data notes

All generated data are synthetic and parameterized for transparency. The design is suitable for demonstration and extension.

## Limitations

The workflow uses simplified mechanisms, simulated parameters, and no validated play, imagination, school-readiness, social-emotional, outdoor-safety, stress, disability-access, or context-quality measurement scale.

## Extension paths

The structure can be extended with validated play-observation instruments, classroom observations, outdoor-play measures, caregiver reports, neighborhood/play-space audits, disability-access variables, peer-network data, digital-play measures, and stronger causal designs.
