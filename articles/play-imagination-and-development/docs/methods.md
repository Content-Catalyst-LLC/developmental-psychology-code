# Methods

## Overview

This workflow creates a synthetic longitudinal panel for play, imagination, and developmental growth research. It demonstrates how play opportunity, symbolic play, social play, material exploration, outdoor embodiment, stress, restriction, peer inclusion, and context-level play ecology can be represented across repeated waves and nested settings.

## Design

The synthetic panel includes:

- child-level baseline development;
- repeated observation waves;
- context-level play infrastructure;
- time-varying pretend play, social play, constructive play, outdoor play, caregiver support, stress, peer inclusion, and play restriction;
- play support context;
- broad cognitive-social-emotional developmental score;
- play-development profiles.

## Modeling strategy

The repository uses synthetic data generation, dynamic regression, multilevel modeling, scenario comparison, SQL summaries, and compiled-language validation utilities.

## Interpretation

The models illustrate play as a developmental process shaped by symbolic imagination, social interaction, material exploration, embodied activity, contextual support, stress, restriction, and institutional opportunity.
