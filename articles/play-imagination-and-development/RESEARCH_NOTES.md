# Research Notes

## Article
Play, Imagination, and Development

## Research frame

This repository operationalizes play as a longitudinal developmental process in which children build cognitive, social, emotional, symbolic, and embodied capacities through pretend play, social play, constructive play, outdoor play, caregiver support, peer inclusion, accessible play space, adult responsiveness, material access, and repeated exploratory practice.

## Core constructs

- development score;
- baseline development;
- pretend play;
- imaginative play;
- social play;
- constructive play;
- outdoor play;
- caregiver support;
- peer inclusion;
- play-space quality;
- adult responsiveness;
- inclusion climate;
- outdoor safety;
- play material access;
- chronic stress;
- play restriction;
- play support context;
- play-development profile.

## Research-grade elements

- synthetic longitudinal child panel data;
- context-level play ecology metadata;
- play and developmental trajectory summaries;
- dynamic state-dependence modeling;
- multilevel play-context modeling;
- scenario comparison for play support, access, stress reduction, inclusion, and outdoor safety;
- SQL schema and analytical queries;
- Python and R workflows;
- compiled-language validation and summary utilities;
- reproducibility, validation, responsible-use, and model-card documentation.
