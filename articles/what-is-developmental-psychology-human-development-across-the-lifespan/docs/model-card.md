# Model Card

## Name

Synthetic developmental lifespan trajectory model.

## Intended use

Research education, article companion materials, methods teaching, trajectory modeling, longitudinal analysis practice, and reproducible workflow development.

## Inputs

Synthetic variables representing baseline regulation, caregiver support, school support, school climate, counseling access, disability support need, disability accommodation, structural risk, acute stress, current support, intervention exposure, and developmental score.

## Outputs

- longitudinal synthetic panel;
- profile summaries;
- school/context summaries;
- dynamic regression outputs;
- trajectory plots;
- protective-context scenario comparisons;
- validation summaries.

## Data notes

All generated data are synthetic and parameterized for transparency. The design is suitable for demonstration and extension, not empirical inference.

## Limitations

- simplified developmental mechanisms;
- simulated parameters;
- no real participant data;
- no causal identification strategy;
- no validated clinical, educational, or developmental measurement scale;
- synthetic school/context structure.

## Extension paths

The structure can be extended with validated measures, cohort data, mixed-method designs, intervention records, school-level administrative data, disability accommodations, multilingual contexts, and stronger causal designs.
