# Research Notes

## Article
What Is Developmental Psychology? Human Development Across the Lifespan

## Research frame

This repository operationalizes developmental psychology as a lifespan science of patterned change and continuity. The framework emphasizes trajectories rather than snapshots, relational and institutional context rather than isolated traits, and probabilistic developmental pathways rather than fixed destinies.

## Core constructs

- developmental score;
- baseline regulation/skill;
- caregiver support;
- family support;
- school support;
- school climate;
- counseling access;
- disability support need;
- disability accommodation;
- structural risk;
- acute stress;
- current support;
- intervention timing;
- protective context;
- school/context clustering;
- developmental trajectory group.

## Research-grade elements

- synthetic longitudinal child-level panel data;
- school/context metadata;
- profile summaries;
- dynamic state-dependence modeling;
- multilevel growth modeling;
- risk/support trajectory comparisons;
- intervention and protective-context scenarios;
- SQL schema and analytical queries;
- Python and R workflows;
- compiled-language validation and summary utilities;
- reproducibility, validation, and model-card documentation.
