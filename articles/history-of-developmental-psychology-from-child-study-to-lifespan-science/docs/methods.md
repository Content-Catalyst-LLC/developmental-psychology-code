# Methods

## Overview

This workflow represents the history of developmental psychology as a synthetic longitudinal field-history panel from 1900 to 2025.

The model includes:

- paradigm shares;
- institutional-support index;
- methodological-advantage index;
- social-relevance index;
- critique index;
- child-centered index;
- lifespan index;
- ecological-systems index;
- broadening index.

## Paradigms

The synthetic panel includes:

- child study;
- maturational theory;
- psychoanalytic theory;
- behaviorism;
- cognitive developmental theory;
- sociocultural theory;
- attachment and social development;
- ecological systems;
- lifespan psychology;
- developmental psychopathology;
- neuroscience and genetics;
- developmental systems.

## Interpretation

The values are synthetic. They are designed to teach how intellectual fields can be modeled as historically changing distributions of attention. They are not empirical publication shares.

## Extension path

To make the workflow empirical, replace the simulated values with real bibliometric, citation, archival, journal, conference, funding, or curriculum data.
