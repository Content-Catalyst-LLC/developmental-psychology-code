# Ethics and Limitations

Life-course inequality models can harm if they turn structural disadvantage into individual deficit.

## Limitations

- Synthetic data only.
- No real persons or communities.
- No validated scale.
- No causal evidence.
- No diagnostic use.
- No ranking of real populations.

## Responsible framing

Use this workflow to illustrate structure, not to label people. Inequality should be interpreted as a distribution of conditions, not a property of persons. Risk is not destiny. Models should preserve attention to support, repair, policy, and institutional responsibility.
