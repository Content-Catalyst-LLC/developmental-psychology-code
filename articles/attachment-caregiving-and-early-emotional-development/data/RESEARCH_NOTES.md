# Research Notes: data

This subfolder supports the companion repository for **Attachment, Caregiving, and Early Emotional Development**.

The workflow models early emotional development as a longitudinal process shaped by caregiving responsiveness, relational repair, caregiver support, childcare continuity, neighborhood safety, family service access, caregiving ecology support, chronic stress, temperament reactivity, disability support need, and caregiving support context.
