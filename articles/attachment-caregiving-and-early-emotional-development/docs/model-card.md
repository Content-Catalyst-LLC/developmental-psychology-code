# Model Card

## Name

Synthetic attachment, caregiving, and early emotional-development trajectory model.

## Intended use

Research education, article companion materials, methods teaching, longitudinal analysis practice, attachment-development modeling, caregiving ecology modeling, and reproducible workflow development.

## Inputs

Synthetic variables representing baseline regulation, caregiving quality, repair capacity, caregiver support, temperament reactivity, disability support need, chronic stress, childcare continuity, neighborhood safety, family service access, caregiving ecology support, current care, current repair, current caregiver support, current stress, caregiving support context, and regulation score.

## Outputs

Longitudinal synthetic panel, profile summaries, context summaries, dynamic regression outputs, trajectory plots, scenario comparisons, and validation summaries.

## Data notes

All generated data are synthetic and parameterized for transparency. The design is suitable for demonstration and extension.

## Limitations

The workflow uses simplified mechanisms, simulated parameters, and no validated attachment classification, Strange Situation observation, caregiver sensitivity measure, stress biomarker, disability-support measure, childcare-quality rating, or early emotional-development assessment.

## Extension paths

The structure can be extended with validated observational measures, caregiver-report scales, attachment interviews, childcare records, service-access data, home visiting exposure, family-support interventions, mixed-method interviews, and stronger causal designs.
