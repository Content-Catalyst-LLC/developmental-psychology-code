# Responsible Use

## Scope

This repository provides transparent synthetic workflows for attachment, caregiving, and early emotional-development trajectory modeling. It is best used for teaching, documentation, reproducible methods, and prototype analysis design.

## Data governance

Any empirical extension should include documented data provenance, privacy protections, infant/child research protections, caregiver consent, disability-rights review, trauma-informed review, cultural humility, anti-bias review, and clear separation between descriptive modeling and causal claims.

## Interpretation

Attachment and early-emotional-development data should be interpreted as contextual and developmental. Scores, profiles, and trajectories should be used to understand systems of care, stress, repair, support, and developmental opportunity rather than to reduce infants, children, or caregivers to fixed categories of worth, competence, or risk.
