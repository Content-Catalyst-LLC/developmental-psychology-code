# Research Notes

## Article
Moral Development and the Growth of Conscience

## Research frame
This repository operationalizes moral development as a longitudinal process in which conscience grows through caregiving guidance, empathy, peer fairness, self-regulation, harm recognition, repair, school moral climate, anti-bullying climate, institutional consistency, digital moral context, and repeated social experience.

## Core constructs
- conscience score
- moral action score
- caregiving guidance
- empathic sensitivity
- peer fairness
- self-regulation
- harm recognition
- repair opportunity
- school moral climate
- restorative practice access
- punitive inconsistency
- anti-bullying climate
- chronic exclusion
- current exclusion
- digital moral safety
- digital cruelty exposure
- peer pressure
- moral support context
- moral development profile
