# Responsible Use

## Scope
This repository provides transparent synthetic workflows for moral-development trajectory modeling. It is best used for teaching, documentation, reproducible methods, and prototype analysis design.

## Data governance
Any empirical extension should include documented data provenance, privacy protections, age-appropriate research design, accessibility considerations, anti-bias review, cultural and religious humility, and clear separation between descriptive modeling and causal claims.

## Interpretation
Moral-development data should be interpreted as contextual and probabilistic. Scores, profiles, and trajectories should be used to understand systems of support, conscience formation, moral climate, and developmental pathways rather than to reduce individuals to fixed moral categories.
