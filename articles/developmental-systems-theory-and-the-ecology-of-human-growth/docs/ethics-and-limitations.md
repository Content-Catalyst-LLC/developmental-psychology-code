# Ethics and Limitations

## Not a child-risk, school-accountability, or clinical prediction tool

This repository must not be used for diagnosis, student prediction, school accountability, child-risk scoring, service eligibility, clinical triage, insurance decisions, surveillance, or any high-stakes decision.

## Key risks

- reducing development to scores;
- treating ecological disadvantage as individual failure;
- using synthetic outputs as empirical evidence;
- mistaking biological sensitivity for fixed destiny;
- ignoring racism, poverty, disability, language access, housing, environmental exposure, and institutional power;
- ranking children, families, schools, neighborhoods, or communities;
- invoking systems language without specifying mechanisms and evidence.

## Responsible use

Use this repository to teach and explore:

- developmental systems theory;
- ecological and bioecological modeling;
- nested person-context data structures;
- reciprocal and path-dependent development;
- multilevel and longitudinal analysis;
- ethical caution in computational developmental science.

Responsible real-world work requires validated measures, consent, privacy protection, careful causal design, community context, institutional review, and attention to power and inequality.
