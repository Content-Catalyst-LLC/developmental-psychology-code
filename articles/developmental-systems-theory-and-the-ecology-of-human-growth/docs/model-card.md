# Model Card

## Name

Synthetic developmental systems theory and ecology of human growth model.

## Intended use

Research education, reproducible-method scaffolding, developmental-systems scenario demonstration, and conceptual modeling.

## Not intended for

Diagnosis, student prediction, child-risk scoring, school accountability, clinical judgment, intervention eligibility, insurance decisions, surveillance, or any high-stakes decision-making.

## Inputs

Synthetic variables representing biological sensitivity, family support, peer belonging, school climate, curriculum opportunity, neighborhood safety, service access, material security, ecological stress, and intervention exposure.

## Outputs

Synthetic development scores, ecological support summaries, ecological stress summaries, and support-profile groupings.

## Limitations

- No empirical validation.
- No causal evidence.
- No child-level predictive validity.
- Synthetic parameter values.
- Simplified nested systems.
- Potential misuse if interpreted as real developmental analytics.

## Ethical position

The model treats development as relational and ecological, not as private merit, family failure, biological fate, or school performance alone. It should never be used to rank or stigmatize real children, families, schools, neighborhoods, or groups.
