# Research Notes

## Article
Adolescence, Identity, and Psychological Transition

## Research frame

Adolescence is modeled as identity reorganization under relational, embodied, digital, and institutional conditions. Identity is not treated as an internal private score; it is shaped by peer recognition, family support, school connectedness, future orientation, identity safety, and exclusion.

## Research-grade elements

- longitudinal synthetic panel data;
- adolescent-level and school-level variables;
- time-varying support, exclusion, digital stress, and future orientation;
- dynamic state-dependence modeling;
- school context summaries;
- scenario analysis for supportive developmental conditions;
- Python, R, SQL, C, C++, Go, Rust, Julia, and Fortran examples;
- explicit ethics, limitations, and reproducibility notes.
