# Research Notes: go

This subfolder supports the research-grade companion workflow for **Adolescence, Identity, and Psychological Transition**.

The workflow models adolescent identity coherence as a synthetic longitudinal process shaped by peer support, family support, school connectedness, future orientation, school climate, counseling access, extracurricular access, identity safety, digital safety, exclusion, and digital stress.

Ethical boundary: these files are for reproducible education and conceptual modeling only. They must not be used for real adolescent screening, surveillance, discipline, placement, mental-health prediction, or identity classification.
