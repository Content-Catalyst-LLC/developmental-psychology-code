# Methods

The workflow simulates adolescents observed across repeated waves and nested within schools. The outcome, `identity_score`, is synthetic identity coherence. Predictors include peer support, family support, school connectedness, future orientation, school climate, counseling access, extracurricular access, identity safety, digital safety, exclusion, and digital stress.

The model includes state dependence: prior identity organization shapes later identity. This reflects the article's argument that adolescence is not simply change over time, but transition under relational and institutional conditions.
