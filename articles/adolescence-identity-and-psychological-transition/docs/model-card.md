# Model Card

## Intended use
Research education, reproducible-method scaffolding, and conceptual modeling of adolescent identity formation.

## Not intended for
Clinical, school-placement, disciplinary, surveillance, identity-classification, or high-stakes use.

## Key variables
Peer support, family support, school connectedness, future orientation, school climate, counseling access, extracurricular access, identity safety, digital safety, exclusion, digital stress, support context, and identity score.

## Limitations
Synthetic data, simplified model structure, no validated measures, no causal evidence, no individual predictive validity.
