# Methods

## Overview

This workflow models disability and neurodivergence as dynamic developmental relations. The synthetic data include children nested within settings and observed repeatedly across time.

The model includes:

- neurodevelopmental or disability-related profile;
- support quality;
- accessibility and accommodation;
- barrier burden;
- communication access;
- caregiver advocacy;
- sensory flexibility;
- service access;
- inclusion climate;
- participation score;
- developmental outcome;
- access-barrier profile.

## Conceptual interpretation

### Profile

`neuro_profile` is not interpreted as deficit. It represents difference or support-relevant profile. The model tests whether context and support shape outcomes around profile.

### Support

`current_support` represents educational, therapeutic, relational, and practical support.

### Access

`current_access` represents the quality of accommodation, assistive technology, and accessible design.

### Barriers

`current_barrier` represents inaccessible design, sensory burden, communication exclusion, rigid institutional practice, or service fragmentation.

### Participation

`participation_score` is modeled directly because participation is not just a byproduct of development. It is a developmental outcome and pathway.

## Synthetic-data caution

The data are not empirical findings. They are simulated for reproducible demonstration only.
