# Ethics and Limitations

## Not a diagnostic or eligibility tool

This repository must not be used for diagnosis, service eligibility, educational placement, insurance decisions, risk scoring, or prediction of real children, families, disabled people, or neurodivergent people.

## Key risks

- reducing disabled or neurodivergent people to scores;
- treating profile as deficit;
- treating lack of participation as personal failure rather than access failure;
- ignoring stigma, masking, poverty, race, language access, gender, geography, and institutional design;
- mistaking synthetic output for evidence;
- using models to justify exclusion.

## Responsible use

Use this repository to teach and explore:

- person-environment fit;
- accessibility and barrier modeling;
- support and participation pathways;
- reproducible analysis;
- synthetic scenario comparison;
- ethical caution in computational developmental psychology.

Responsible real-world work requires validated measures, consent, privacy protections, lived-experience input, disability expertise, and institutional accountability.
