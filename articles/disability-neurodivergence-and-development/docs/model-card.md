# Model Card

## Name

Synthetic disability, neurodivergence, access, and developmental participation model.

## Intended use

Research education, reproducible-method scaffolding, accessibility scenario demonstration, and conceptual modeling.

## Not intended for

Diagnosis, service eligibility, educational placement, individualized prediction, insurance decisions, school discipline, clinical triage, or allocation of real services.

## Inputs

Synthetic variables representing support, access, barrier burden, communication access, caregiver advocacy, participation, inclusion climate, sensory flexibility, and service access.

## Outputs

Synthetic development and participation scores.

## Limitations

- No empirical validation.
- No clinical validity.
- No causal evidence.
- Synthetic parameter values.
- Simplified access and barrier structure.
- Potential misuse if interpreted as prediction.

## Ethical position

The model treats disability and neurodivergence relationally. It should never be used to rank or stigmatize real people.
