# Research Notes: fortran

This subfolder is part of the research-grade companion directory for:

**Disability, Neurodivergence, and Development**

## Function of this folder

The `fortran/` folder contributes to a reproducible, multi-language research workflow for modeling disability, neurodivergence, access, barriers, support, communication, participation, and developmental outcomes over time.

## Article directory

`articles/disability-neurodivergence-and-development`

## Research commitments

- disability as developmental relation;
- neurodivergence as part of human developmental diversity;
- participation as a developmental outcome;
- accessibility and accommodation as developmental variables;
- barrier burden as a measurable contextual condition;
- ethical caution against diagnostic, eligibility, or punitive misuse;
- transparent notes and schemas.
