# Ethics and Limitations

## Not a student prediction system

This repository must not be used for student ranking, school accountability, disciplinary prediction, resource denial, teacher evaluation, clinical judgment, or any high-stakes educational decision.

## Key risks

- reducing students to scores;
- treating school outcomes as individual merit alone;
- ignoring structural inequality and opportunity differences;
- using synthetic output as evidence;
- ranking schools, students, teachers, or communities;
- ignoring disability, language access, racism, poverty, and family burden;
- using models to justify exclusion or surveillance.

## Responsible use

Use this repository to teach and explore:

- schooling as developmental formation;
- school connectedness and support;
- curriculum opportunity and resource capacity;
- synthetic longitudinal modeling;
- multilevel school structure;
- ethical caution in computational education research.

Responsible real-world work requires validated measures, consent, privacy protection, careful causal design, institutional review, and attention to structural inequality.
