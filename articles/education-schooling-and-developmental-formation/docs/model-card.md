# Model Card

## Name

Synthetic schooling, connectedness, and developmental formation model.

## Intended use

Research education, reproducible-method scaffolding, school-climate scenario demonstration, and conceptual modeling.

## Not intended for

Student prediction, accountability, disciplinary decisions, teacher evaluation, school ranking, intervention eligibility, clinical judgment, or any high-stakes decision-making.

## Inputs

Synthetic variables representing teacher support, peer belonging, school climate, curriculum opportunity, school stress, family support, resource capacity, restorative practice, and intervention exposure.

## Outputs

Synthetic connectedness and development scores.

## Limitations

- No empirical validation.
- No causal evidence.
- No student-level predictive validity.
- Synthetic parameter values.
- Simplified school structure.
- Potential misuse if interpreted as real education analytics.

## Ethical position

The model treats schooling outcomes as ecological and institutional, not as private merit alone. It should never be used to rank or stigmatize real students, teachers, schools, or communities.
