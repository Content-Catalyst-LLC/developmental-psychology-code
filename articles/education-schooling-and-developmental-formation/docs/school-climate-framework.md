# School Climate and Developmental Formation Framework

## Developmental school variables

| Domain | Developmental relevance |
|---|---|
| Teacher support | Recognition, scaffolding, care, feedback, adult trust |
| Peer belonging | Safety, friendship, identity, social comparison, engagement |
| School climate | Institutional trust, safety, discipline, support, dignity |
| Curriculum opportunity | Cognitive development, aspiration, skill, symbolic access |
| Family support | Home-school continuity, routines, advocacy, encouragement |
| School stress | Anxiety, disengagement, avoidance, dysregulation, burnout |
| Restorative practice | Repair, accountability, relationship, reduced exclusion |
| Resource capacity | Instructional quality, support availability, enrichment |

## Interpretation

The workflow does not ask whether students are simply high- or low-achieving. It asks how developmental outcomes change when connection, curriculum, climate, and support change.
