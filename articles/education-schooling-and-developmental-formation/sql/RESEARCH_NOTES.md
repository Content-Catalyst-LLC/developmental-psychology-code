# Research Notes: sql

This subfolder is part of the research-grade companion directory for:

**Education, Schooling, and Developmental Formation**

## Function of this folder

The `sql/` folder contributes to a reproducible, multi-language research workflow for modeling schooling as developmental formation: teacher support, peer belonging, school climate, curriculum opportunity, stress, connectedness, intervention exposure, and student development over time.

## Article directory

`articles/education-schooling-and-developmental-formation`

## Research commitments

- schooling as a developmental institution;
- school connectedness as developmental infrastructure;
- teacher and peer relationships as pathway variables;
- curriculum opportunity and resource capacity as institutional conditions;
- school stress and discipline climate as developmental burdens;
- ethical caution against student prediction or high-stakes misuse;
- transparent notes and schemas.
