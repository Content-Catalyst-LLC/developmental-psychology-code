# Research Notes

## Article
Self-Regulation and Executive Function Across Development

## Research frame

This repository operationalizes self-regulation and executive function as longitudinal developmental capacities shaped by co-regulation, caregiving support, classroom structure, sleep quality, school climate, regulation scaffolding, transition predictability, disability accommodation, intervention exposure, chronic stress, acute stress, temperament reactivity, and repeated regulatory practice.

## Core constructs

- regulation score;
- baseline executive function;
- caregiving support;
- classroom structure;
- sleep quality;
- school climate;
- regulation scaffolding;
- transition predictability;
- disability support need;
- disability accommodation;
- chronic stress;
- acute stress;
- temperament reactivity;
- intervention exposure;
- regulatory support context;
- regulation profile.

## Research-grade elements

- synthetic longitudinal child/adolescent panel data;
- school/context metadata;
- regulation and support trajectories;
- dynamic state-dependence modeling;
- multilevel regulation modeling;
- scenario comparison for support, stress, sleep, structure, and intervention;
- SQL schema and analytical queries;
- Python and R workflows;
- compiled-language validation and summary utilities;
- reproducibility, validation, responsible-use, and model-card documentation.
