# Research Notes: docs

This subfolder is part of the research-grade companion directory for:

**Aging, Adaptation, and Development in Later Life**

## Function of this folder

The `docs/` folder contributes to a reproducible, multi-language research workflow for modeling later-life development: functional ability, social support, health burden, adaptive strategy, meaning orientation, environmental accessibility, dignity support, service access, functional fit, adjustment, care context, and developmental change over time.

## Article directory

`articles/aging-adaptation-and-development-in-later-life`

## Research commitments

- aging as ongoing development;
- adaptation as active reorganization;
- functional ability as person-environment fit;
- health burden as developmental condition, not total identity;
- dignity, care systems, environmental accessibility, and service access as developmental supports;
- ethical caution against high-stakes prediction or deficit labeling;
- transparent notes and schemas.
