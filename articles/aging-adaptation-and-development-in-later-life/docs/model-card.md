# Model Card

## Name

Synthetic aging, adaptation, and later-life development model.

## Intended use

Research education, reproducible-method scaffolding, later-life development scenario demonstration, and conceptual modeling.

## Not intended for

Clinical diagnosis, fall-risk prediction, cognitive screening, elder-care eligibility, benefits decisions, insurance decisions, surveillance, or any high-stakes decision-making.

## Inputs

Synthetic variables representing functional ability, social support, health burden, adaptive strategy, meaning orientation, environmental accessibility, dignity support, service access, care context, and functional fit.

## Outputs

Synthetic adjustment scores, functional-fit summaries, health-burden trajectories, adaptation profiles, and scenario comparisons.

## Limitations

- No empirical validation.
- No causal evidence.
- No person-level predictive validity.
- No validated clinical instrument.
- Synthetic parameter values.
- Simplified later-life and care-context structure.
- Potential misuse if interpreted as real aging analytics.

## Ethical position

The model treats older adults as persons with dignity, agency, vulnerability, history, support needs, and meaning. It should never be used to rank or stigmatize real older adults, families, caregivers, disabled people, care settings, communities, or institutions.
