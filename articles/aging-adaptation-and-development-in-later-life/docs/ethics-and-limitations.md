# Ethics and Limitations

## Not a clinical aging, fall-risk, cognitive, insurance, or elder-care prediction tool

This repository must not be used for diagnosis, fall-risk prediction, cognitive screening, care eligibility, insurance decisions, benefits decisions, clinical triage, surveillance, or any high-stakes decision.

## Key risks

- reducing older adults to adjustment or health-burden scores;
- treating aging as decline-only;
- treating adaptation as a substitute for adequate care or accessible environments;
- ignoring poverty, racism, ageism, disability, caregiving burden, housing, health care, and institutional quality;
- mistaking synthetic output for empirical evidence;
- ranking individuals, care settings, neighborhoods, or institutions;
- using resilience language to excuse neglect or underinvestment.

## Responsible use

Use this repository to teach and explore:

- aging as continuing development;
- healthy aging and functional ability;
- person-environment fit;
- compensation and adaptive strategy;
- health burden and social support;
- dignity and care context;
- synthetic longitudinal modeling.

Responsible real-world work requires validated measures, clinical and gerontological expertise, privacy protection, careful causal design, institutional review, and attention to dignity, ageism, disability, caregiving, and inequality.
