# Research Notes: c

This subfolder is part of the research-grade companion directory for:

**Genes, Environment, and Developmental Plasticity**

## Function of this folder

The `c/` folder contributes to a reproducible, multi-language research workflow for modeling biological sensitivity, environmental exposure, timing, biological embedding, stress, support, nutrition, school context, service access, and developmental plasticity over time.

## Article directory

`articles/genes-environment-and-developmental-plasticity`

## Research commitments

- genes as developmental potential, not fixed destiny;
- environment as developmental condition;
- gene-environment interaction and correlation as coaction;
- plasticity as conditional openness;
- timing-sensitive exposure and biological embedding;
- ethical caution against genetic, clinical, or policy prediction misuse;
- transparent notes and schemas.
