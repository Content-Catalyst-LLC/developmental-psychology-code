# Ethics and Limitations

## Not a clinical tool

This repository must not be used for diagnosis, clinical triage, service allocation, disciplinary decisions, or prediction of real children or families.

## Key risks

- reducing children to risk scores;
- treating diagnosis as destiny;
- pathologizing families or communities;
- ignoring poverty, disability, racism, trauma, exclusion, and institutional harm;
- mistaking synthetic output for evidence;
- using models for punitive decisions.

## Responsible use

Use this repository to teach and explore:

- developmental pathways;
- risk and protective factors;
- model structure;
- reproducible analysis;
- synthetic scenario comparison;
- ethical caution in computational psychology.

Responsible real-world work requires validated measures, consent, privacy protections, domain expertise, clinical review where appropriate, and community accountability.
