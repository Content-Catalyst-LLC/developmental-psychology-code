# Model Card

## Name

Synthetic developmental psychopathology pathway model.

## Intended use

Research education, reproducible-method scaffolding, and conceptual demonstration.

## Not intended for

Diagnosis, screening, prediction, risk scoring, eligibility decisions, discipline, custody, insurance, or clinical decision-making.

## Inputs

Synthetic variables representing regulation, risk, support, stability, biological sensitivity, school belonging, service access, and community support.

## Outputs

Synthetic adaptation, internalizing, and externalizing scores.

## Limitations

- No empirical validation.
- No clinical validity.
- No causal evidence.
- Synthetic parameter values.
- Simplified pathway structure.
- Potential misuse if interpreted as prediction.

## Ethical position

The model treats risk as contextual and probabilistic. It should never be used to rank or stigmatize real people.
