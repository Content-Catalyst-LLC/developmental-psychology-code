# Research Notes: rust

This subfolder is part of the research-grade companion directory for:

**Developmental Psychopathology: Risk, Resilience, and Adaptation**

## Function of this folder

The `rust/` folder contributes to a reproducible, multi-language research workflow for modeling developmental psychopathology as a dynamic pathway process.

## Article directory

`articles/developmental-psychopathology-risk-resilience-and-adaptation`

## Research commitments

- pathway modeling rather than static labeling;
- cumulative risk and protective support;
- adaptation, internalizing, and externalizing trajectories;
- reproducibility across languages;
- ethical caution against diagnostic or punitive misuse;
- transparent notes and schemas.
