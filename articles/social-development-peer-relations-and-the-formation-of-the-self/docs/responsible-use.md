# Responsible Use

## Scope

This repository provides transparent synthetic workflows for social-development trajectory modeling. It is best used for teaching, documentation, reproducible methods, and prototype analysis design.

## Data governance

Any empirical extension should include documented data provenance, privacy protections, age-appropriate research design, accessibility considerations, anti-bias review, cultural and linguistic humility, and clear separation between descriptive modeling and causal claims.

## Interpretation

Social-development data should be interpreted as contextual and probabilistic. Scores, profiles, and trajectories should be used to understand systems of support, peer relations, school climate, connectedness, and developmental pathways rather than to reduce individuals to fixed social categories.
