# Research Notes: julia

This subfolder is part of the research-grade companion directory for:

**Parenting, Family Systems, and Human Development**

## Function of this folder

The `julia/` folder contributes to a reproducible, multi-language research workflow for modeling parenting and family systems: parenting responsiveness, family climate, household stability, kin support, sibling support, child regulation, caregiver support, stress, and developmental outcomes over time.

## Article directory

`articles/parenting-family-systems-and-human-development`

## Research commitments

- parenting as relational development;
- family systems as nested developmental ecologies;
- caregiver support as child-development support;
- stress as ecological and contextual, not merely individual failure;
- family climate and repair as developmental conditions;
- ethical caution against family-risk scoring or high-stakes misuse;
- transparent notes and schemas.
