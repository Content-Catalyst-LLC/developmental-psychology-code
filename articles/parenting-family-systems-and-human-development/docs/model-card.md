# Model Card

## Name

Synthetic parenting, family systems, and human development model.

## Intended use

Research education, reproducible-method scaffolding, family-systems scenario demonstration, and conceptual modeling.

## Not intended for

Parenting evaluation, custody decisions, child-protective prediction, family-risk scoring, clinical diagnosis, resource eligibility, surveillance, or any high-stakes decision-making.

## Inputs

Synthetic variables representing parenting responsiveness, family climate, household stability, kin support, sibling support, child regulation, caregiver support, external stress, and economic security.

## Outputs

Synthetic development and family support scores.

## Limitations

- No empirical validation.
- No causal evidence.
- No family-level predictive validity.
- Synthetic parameter values.
- Simplified household structure.
- Potential misuse if interpreted as real family analytics.

## Ethical position

The model treats family outcomes as relational and ecological, not as private parental merit or failure alone. It should never be used to rank or stigmatize real caregivers, children, families, or communities.
