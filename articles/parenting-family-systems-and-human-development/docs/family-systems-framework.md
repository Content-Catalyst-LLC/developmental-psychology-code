# Family Systems and Developmental Formation Framework

## Developmental family variables

| Domain | Developmental relevance |
|---|---|
| Parenting responsiveness | Co-regulation, safety, guidance, attunement, trust |
| Family climate | Emotional tone, conflict, warmth, communication, repair |
| Household stability | Routine, predictability, continuity, organized care |
| Kin support | Extended support, buffering, caregiving backup, cultural continuity |
| Sibling support | Companionship, role learning, cooperation, rivalry, protection |
| Child regulation | Temperament, self-regulation, feedback into family dynamics |
| Caregiver support | Respite, coaching, concrete support, caregiver wellbeing |
| External stress | Economic pressure, housing strain, health burden, institutional pressure |

## Interpretation

The workflow does not ask whether parents are simply good or bad. It asks how developmental outcomes change when responsiveness, family climate, support, stability, and stress change.
