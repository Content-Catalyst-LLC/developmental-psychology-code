# Ethics and Limitations

This repository must not be used for puberty classification, clinical diagnosis, mental-health screening, gender or sexuality classification, disability classification, school placement, student discipline, body-image-risk scoring, adolescent ranking, surveillance, eligibility decisions, or any high-stakes decision.

The data are synthetic. The outputs are not empirical findings. The workflow is designed to model puberty as biological and embodied while emphasizing privacy, dignity, support, disability accommodation, menstrual support, anti-harassment protections, and developmental care.
