# Model Card

## Intended use
Research education, reproducible-method scaffolding, and conceptual modeling of puberty, embodiment, and adolescent transition.

## Not intended for
Clinical diagnosis, puberty assessment, mental-health screening, school placement, discipline, body ranking, gender/sexuality classification, disability classification, adolescent surveillance, or high-stakes decision-making.

## Key variables
Pubertal progression, timing deviation, family support, peer comparison, body concern, stigma, digital visibility, school support, health education, privacy protection, menstrual support, disability accommodation, anti-harassment climate, protective context, and adjustment score.

## Limitations
Synthetic data, simplified model structure, no validated measures, no causal evidence, and no individual predictive validity.
