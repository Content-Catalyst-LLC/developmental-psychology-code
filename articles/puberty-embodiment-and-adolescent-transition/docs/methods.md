# Methods

This workflow simulates adolescents observed across repeated waves and nested within schools. The outcome, `adjustment_score`, is a synthetic embodiment-adjustment measure. Predictors include pubertal progression, timing deviation, family support, peer comparison, body-image vulnerability, current body concern, stigma, digital visibility, school support, health education, privacy protection, menstrual support, disability accommodation, anti-harassment climate, and protective context.

The design is intentionally synthetic. It is meant to make the article's developmental argument reproducible and teachable, not to estimate real pubertal, clinical, or school effects.
