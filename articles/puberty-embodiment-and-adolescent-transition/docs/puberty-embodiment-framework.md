# Puberty and Embodiment Framework

| Construct | Developmental meaning |
|---|---|
| Pubertal progression | Synthetic bodily maturation across waves |
| Timing deviation | Relative pubertal timing compared with peers |
| Family support | Communication, privacy, and emotional support |
| Peer comparison | Body-based comparison and status pressure |
| Body concern | Time-varying body-image concern |
| Stigma | Shame, harassment, adultification, or exclusion |
| Digital visibility | Online exposure, comparison, and platform stress |
| School support | Institutional support for adolescent well-being |
| Health education | Puberty and body education quality |
| Privacy protection | Bathroom, locker-room, medical, and bodily privacy |
| Menstrual support | Product access, pain support, and dignity |
| Disability accommodation | Supports for disabled and neurodivergent adolescents |
| Anti-harassment climate | Protection from bullying and body-based harm |
| Protective context | Composite developmental infrastructure |
