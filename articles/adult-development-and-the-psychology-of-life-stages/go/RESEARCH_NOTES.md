# Research Notes: go

This subfolder is part of the research-grade companion directory for:

**Adult Development and the Psychology of Life Stages**

## Function of this folder

The `go/` folder contributes to a reproducible, multi-language research workflow for modeling adult development: relational support, work integration, health burden, adaptive resources, role burden, institutional support, community stability, life stage, context, and developmental adjustment over time.

## Article directory

`articles/adult-development-and-the-psychology-of-life-stages`

## Research commitments

- adulthood as continuing development;
- life stages as contextual and historically variable, not rigid scripts;
- adult adjustment as relational, embodied, institutional, and cumulative;
- role burden, caregiving, work, and health as developmental conditions;
- ethical caution against high-stakes prediction or deficit labeling;
- transparent notes and schemas.
