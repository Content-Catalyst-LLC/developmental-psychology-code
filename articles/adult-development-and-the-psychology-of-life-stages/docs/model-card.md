# Model Card

## Name

Synthetic adult development and life-stages model.

## Intended use

Research education, reproducible-method scaffolding, adult-development scenario demonstration, and conceptual modeling.

## Not intended for

Clinical diagnosis, mental-health screening, hiring, employee performance assessment, workplace surveillance, benefits decisions, insurance decisions, or any high-stakes decision-making.

## Inputs

Synthetic variables representing life stage, relational support, work integration, health burden, adaptive resources, role burden, institutional support, community stability, and adult adjustment.

## Outputs

Synthetic adjustment scores, life-stage trajectories, support/burden summaries, adult-development profiles, and scenario comparisons.

## Limitations

- No empirical validation.
- No causal evidence.
- No person-level predictive validity.
- No validated clinical or workplace instrument.
- Synthetic parameter values.
- Simplified adult life-stage and context structure.
- Potential misuse if interpreted as real adult analytics.

## Ethical position

The model treats adults as persons with dignity, relationships, obligations, histories, health conditions, vulnerabilities, and agency. It should never be used to rank or stigmatize real adults, workers, caregivers, families, disabled people, older adults, workplaces, communities, or institutions.
