# Ethics and Limitations

## Not a clinical, workplace, benefits, insurance, hiring, or mental-health prediction tool

This repository must not be used for diagnosis, employee screening, hiring decisions, insurance decisions, benefits eligibility, mental-health triage, performance management, surveillance, or any high-stakes decision.

## Key risks

- reducing adults to adjustment or productivity scores;
- treating life-stage timing as a moral norm;
- treating marriage, parenting, work, retirement, or independent living as required developmental milestones;
- ignoring poverty, racism, disability, gendered care burden, migration, ageism, housing, health care, and labor conditions;
- mistaking synthetic output for empirical evidence;
- ranking people, workplaces, communities, or institutions;
- using resilience language to excuse exploitative work, inadequate care, or social neglect.

## Responsible use

Use this repository to teach and explore:

- adult development as lifelong change;
- young adulthood, midlife, and later adulthood as contextual stages;
- relational support and work integration;
- health burden and role burden;
- adaptive resources and institutional support;
- synthetic longitudinal modeling.

Responsible real-world work requires validated measures, domain expertise, privacy protection, careful causal design, institutional review, and attention to dignity, inequality, disability, care burden, work conditions, and context.
