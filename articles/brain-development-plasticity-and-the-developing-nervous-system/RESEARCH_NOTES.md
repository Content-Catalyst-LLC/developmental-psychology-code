# Research Notes

## Article
Brain Development, Plasticity, and the Developing Nervous System

## Research frame
This repository treats brain development as structured, plastic, embodied, and ecological. The synthetic workflow models how neural state and developmental functioning may change across repeated waves under differing conditions of family support, learning opportunity, sleep, sensory regulation, school support, neighborhood safety, health-service access, environmental risk, acute stress, and chronic stress.

## Core constructs
neural state; developmental outcome; baseline neural state; family support; learning context; sleep quality; sensory regulation support; school support; neighborhood safety; health-service access; environmental risk; acute stress; chronic stress; developmental support context; neurodevelopmental profile.

## Research-grade elements
Synthetic longitudinal data; nonlinear state-dependent modeling; multilevel context structure; scenario comparison; SQL schema and queries; Python and R workflows; compiled-language validation and summaries; reproducibility, validation, responsible-use, and model-card documentation.
