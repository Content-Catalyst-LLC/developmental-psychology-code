# Model Card

## Name
Synthetic brain development, neural plasticity, and developmental-outcome trajectory model.

## Intended use
Research education, article companion materials, methods teaching, longitudinal analysis practice, neurodevelopmental modeling, developmental ecology modeling, and reproducible workflow development.

## Inputs
Synthetic variables representing baseline neural state, family support, learning context, sleep quality, sensory regulation support, chronic stress, school support, neighborhood safety, health-service access, environmental risk, current family support, current learning, current sleep, current sensory support, acute stress, developmental support context, neural state, and developmental outcome.

## Outputs
Longitudinal synthetic panel, profile summaries, context summaries, dynamic regression outputs, nonlinear growth outputs, trajectory plots, scenario comparisons, and validation summaries.

## Limitations
This workflow uses synthetic parameters and no neuroimaging, biomarker, clinical neurological measure, validated stress scale, sleep measure, sensory assessment, environmental exposure measure, or diagnostic instrument.
