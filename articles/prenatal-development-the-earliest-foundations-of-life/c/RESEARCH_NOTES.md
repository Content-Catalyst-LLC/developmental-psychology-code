# Research Notes: c

This subfolder is part of the research-grade companion directory for:

**Prenatal Development: The Earliest Foundations of Life**

## Function of this folder

The `c/` folder contributes to a reproducible, multi-language research workflow for modeling prenatal development: gestational timing, maternal health, prenatal care, nutrition support, social support, chronic stress, toxic exposure, healthcare access, environmental burden, economic security, effective care, developmental risk, neighborhood context, and early developmental outcome.

## Article directory

`articles/prenatal-development-the-earliest-foundations-of-life`

## Research commitments

- prenatal development as the first developmental environment;
- timing and sensitive-period vulnerability as developmental mechanisms;
- maternal health and fetal development as relationally mediated;
- care, nutrition, support, environment, and inequality as developmental conditions;
- ethical caution against maternal blame, surveillance, or high-stakes prediction;
- transparent notes and schemas.
