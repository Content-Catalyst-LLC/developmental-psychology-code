# Ethics and Limitations

## Not a clinical aging, cognitive prediction, or life-outcome scoring tool

This repository must not be used for diagnosis, cognitive screening, elder-care eligibility, insurance decisions, benefits decisions, employment decisions, health-risk scoring, service eligibility, surveillance, or any high-stakes decision.

## Key risks

- reducing persons to developmental scores;
- treating aging as decline-only;
- treating compensation as a substitute for adequate support;
- ignoring poverty, disability, gender, racism, ageism, housing, care access, health care, and institutional inequality;
- mistaking synthetic output for empirical evidence;
- ranking individuals, cohorts, communities, or institutions;
- using plasticity language to blame people for not adapting under poor conditions.

## Responsible use

Use this repository to teach and explore:

- lifespan developmental psychology;
- Baltes's propositions;
- gains and losses;
- plasticity and constraint;
- selective optimization with compensation;
- cohort and institutional context;
- synthetic longitudinal modeling.

Responsible real-world work requires validated measures, domain expertise, privacy protection, careful causal design, institutional review, and attention to power, dignity, ageism, disability, and inequality.
