# Research Notes: outputs

This subfolder is part of the research-grade companion directory for:

**Stage Theories of Development: Promise, Power, and Critique**

## Function of this folder

The `outputs/` folder contributes to a reproducible, multi-language workflow for modeling stage-like development: continuous growth, developmental thresholds, transition readiness, stage-like reorganization, logistic transition, contextual support, chronic stress, resource stability, and outcome trajectories over time.

## Article directory

`articles/stage-theories-of-development-promise-power-and-critique`

## Research commitments

- stage theories as heuristic maps, not rigid scripts;
- transition timing as variable and context-sensitive;
- stage-like change as a testable shape of development;
- support, stress, institutions, culture, disability, and inequality as developmental conditions;
- ethical caution against ranking, screening, or universalizing narrow sequences;
- transparent notes and schemas.
