# Ethics and Limitations

## Not a screening, placement, ranking, disability, maturity, or clinical tool

This repository must not be used for developmental screening, school placement, disability classification, maturity scoring, clinical diagnosis, risk prediction, eligibility decisions, child ranking, student tracking, surveillance, or any high-stakes decision.

## Key risks

- treating stage models as universal scripts;
- ranking children, adolescents, adults, cultures, disabled people, or neurodivergent people by an idealized developmental staircase;
- confusing cultural privilege with developmental maturity;
- ignoring context, language, disability, neurodivergence, schooling, poverty, trauma, migration, and institutional inequality;
- mistaking synthetic output for empirical evidence;
- using stage language to deny opportunity or support.

## Responsible use

Use this repository to teach and explore:

- the difference between continuous growth and stage-like reorganization;
- threshold and logistic models of development;
- context-sensitive transition timing;
- the critique of rigid stage theory;
- synthetic longitudinal modeling.

Responsible real-world research requires validated measures, longitudinal design, cultural humility, disability rights awareness, careful causal inference, anti-bias safeguards, and attention to developmental dignity.
