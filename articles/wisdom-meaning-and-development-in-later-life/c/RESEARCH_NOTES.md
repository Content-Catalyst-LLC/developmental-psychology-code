# Research Notes: c

This subfolder is part of the research-grade companion directory for:

**Wisdom, Meaning, and Development in Later Life**

## Function of this folder

The `c/` folder contributes to a reproducible, multi-language research workflow for modeling later-life wisdom and meaning: social connection, reflective integration, health burden, adaptive support, legacy orientation, dignity support, service access, community participation, wisdom index, meaning score, care context, and developmental change over time.

## Article directory

`articles/wisdom-meaning-and-development-in-later-life`

## Research commitments

- later life as developmentally active;
- wisdom as interpretive and relational rather than automatic with age;
- meaning as path-dependent and context-sensitive;
- health burden as developmental condition, not total identity;
- dignity, care systems, service access, and community participation as developmental supports;
- ethical caution against high-stakes prediction or deficit labeling;
- transparent notes and schemas.
