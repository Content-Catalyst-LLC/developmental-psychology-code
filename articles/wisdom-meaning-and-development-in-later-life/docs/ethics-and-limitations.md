# Ethics and Limitations

## Not a clinical aging, mental-health, loneliness, dementia, or elder-care prediction tool

This repository must not be used for diagnosis, cognitive screening, depression screening, loneliness scoring, elder-care eligibility, insurance decisions, benefits decisions, clinical triage, surveillance, or any high-stakes decision.

## Key risks

- reducing older adults to meaning or wisdom scores;
- treating wisdom as automatic with age;
- treating loneliness, illness, or disability as personal failure;
- ignoring poverty, racism, ageism, disability, caregiving burden, housing, care access, and institutional quality;
- mistaking synthetic output for empirical evidence;
- ranking individuals, care settings, communities, or institutions;
- using adaptation language to excuse inadequate care or social neglect.

## Responsible use

Use this repository to teach and explore:

- later-life meaning;
- wisdom and reflective integration;
- social connection and loneliness;
- health burden and adaptive support;
- dignity and care context;
- legacy orientation and intergenerational meaning;
- synthetic longitudinal modeling.

Responsible real-world work requires validated measures, domain expertise, privacy protection, careful causal design, community context, institutional review, and attention to dignity, ageism, disability, caregiving, and inequality.
