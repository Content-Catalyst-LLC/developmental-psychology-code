# Model Card

## Name

Synthetic wisdom, meaning, and later-life development model.

## Intended use

Research education, reproducible-method scaffolding, later-life development scenario demonstration, and conceptual modeling.

## Not intended for

Clinical diagnosis, depression screening, loneliness scoring, cognitive screening, elder-care eligibility, benefits decisions, insurance decisions, surveillance, or any high-stakes decision-making.

## Inputs

Synthetic variables representing social connection, reflective integration, health burden, adaptive support, legacy orientation, dignity support, service access, community participation, care context, wisdom, and meaning.

## Outputs

Synthetic meaning scores, wisdom-index summaries, connection trajectories, care-context summaries, and meaning-profile groupings.

## Limitations

- No empirical validation.
- No causal evidence.
- No person-level predictive validity.
- No validated wisdom or meaning instrument.
- Synthetic parameter values.
- Simplified later-life and care-context structure.
- Potential misuse if interpreted as real aging analytics.

## Ethical position

The model treats older adults as persons with dignity, history, relationships, agency, vulnerability, and meaning. It should never be used to rank or stigmatize real older adults, families, caregivers, care settings, communities, disabled people, or institutions.
