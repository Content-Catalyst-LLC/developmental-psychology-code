# Responsible Use

## Scope

This repository provides transparent synthetic workflows for language-development trajectory modeling. It is best used for teaching, documentation, reproducible methods, and prototype analysis design.

## Data governance

Any empirical extension should include documented data provenance, privacy protections, child research protections, caregiver consent, disability-rights review, language-access review, cultural humility, anti-bias review, and clear separation between descriptive modeling and causal claims.

## Interpretation

Language-development data should be interpreted as contextual and developmental. Scores, profiles, and trajectories should be used to understand systems of interaction, hearing support, language access, multilingual recognition, schooling, stress, and communication opportunity rather than to reduce children, families, dialects, accents, sign systems, or communication modes to fixed categories of deficiency.
