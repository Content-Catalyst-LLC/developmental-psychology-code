# Research Notes: r

This subfolder is part of the research-grade companion directory for:

**Nature, Nurture, and the Developmental Question**

## Function of this folder

The `r/` folder contributes to a reproducible, multi-language workflow for modeling nature, nurture, and developmental systems: biological sensitivity, gene-environment interaction, differential susceptibility, caregiver support, acute stress, structural risk, chronic adversity, institutional support, disability support, resource stability, intervention exposure, protective context, and developmental trajectories over time.

## Article directory

`articles/nature-nurture-and-the-developmental-question`

## Research commitments

- nature and nurture as interacting developmental systems;
- biological sensitivity as context-responsive rather than deterministic;
- nurture as family, school, institution, culture, policy, and material condition;
- structural risk and inequality as developmental conditions;
- ethical caution against genetic determinism, family blame, surveillance, ranking, or high-stakes prediction;
- transparent notes and schemas.
