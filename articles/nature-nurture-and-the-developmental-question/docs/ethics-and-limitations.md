# Ethics and Limitations

## Not a genetic, diagnostic, placement, ranking, or prediction tool

This repository must not be used for genetic screening, clinical diagnosis, disability classification, school placement, child ranking, student tracking, family-risk scoring, surveillance, eligibility decisions, mental-health prediction, or any high-stakes decision.

## Key risks

- treating biological sensitivity as genetic destiny;
- treating environmental explanation as parental blame;
- using synthetic developmental scores to rank real children, families, schools, or communities;
- ignoring disability, neurodivergence, culture, poverty, racism, environmental exposure, language access, and institutional inequality;
- treating resilience as individual toughness while ignoring support systems;
- mistaking synthetic output for empirical evidence;
- using nature-nurture language to justify exclusion, lowered expectations, or social hierarchy.

## Responsible use

Use this repository to teach and explore:

- gene-environment interaction;
- differential susceptibility;
- structural risk and protective context;
- path dependence;
- nested institutional context;
- synthetic longitudinal modeling.

Responsible real-world research requires validated measures, developmental expertise, privacy protection, consent and assent safeguards, anti-bias procedures, disability rights awareness, cultural humility, careful causal inference, and respect for dignity and safety.
