# Research Notes: notebooks

This subfolder is part of the research-grade companion directory for:

**Gender Development and Sexual Development**

## Function of this folder

The `notebooks/` folder contributes to a reproducible, multi-language research workflow for modeling gender development and sexual development as distinct but interacting developmental processes. The workflow includes pubertal progression, family support, social recognition, consent knowledge, school connectedness, school climate, health education quality, anti-harassment support, stigma exposure, protective context, and broad adolescent adjustment over time.

## Article directory

`articles/gender-development-and-sexual-development`

## Research commitments

- gender development, sex development, sexual development, and sexual orientation are related but not identical;
- embodiment and puberty matter, but bodies are interpreted socially;
- consent knowledge and accurate sexual-health information are protective;
- stigma, misinformation, harassment, and coercion are contextual risks;
- family support, school connectedness, recognition, health education, and anti-harassment supports are protective conditions;
- ethical caution against surveillance, ranking, or high-stakes prediction;
- transparent notes and schemas.
