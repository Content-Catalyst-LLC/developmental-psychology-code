# Model Card

## Name

Synthetic gender development, sexual development, protective context, and adolescent adjustment model.

## Intended use

Research education, reproducible-method scaffolding, support/stigma scenario demonstration, and conceptual modeling.

## Not intended for

Clinical diagnosis, identity screening, sexual orientation inference, gender identity inference, school discipline, student surveillance, outing, child-welfare decisions, mental-health prediction, placement decisions, or any high-stakes decision-making.

## Inputs

Synthetic variables representing pubertal progression, family support, social recognition, consent knowledge, school connectedness, school climate, health education quality, anti-harassment support, stigma exposure, chronic stigma, protective context, and adolescent adjustment.

## Outputs

Synthetic adjustment scores, protective-context summaries, stigma trajectories, adolescent-development profiles, school summaries, and scenario comparisons.

## Limitations

- No empirical validation.
- No causal evidence.
- No person-level predictive validity.
- No validated clinical, educational, or psychological instrument.
- Synthetic parameter values.
- Simplified school and adolescent development structure.
- Potential misuse if interpreted as real adolescent analytics.

## Ethical position

The model treats young people as developing persons with dignity, privacy, vulnerability, agency, and rights. It should never be used to rank, stigmatize, surveil, out, discipline, or predict real adolescents, families, schools, disabled young people, LGBTQ+ young people, religious communities, or marginalized groups.
