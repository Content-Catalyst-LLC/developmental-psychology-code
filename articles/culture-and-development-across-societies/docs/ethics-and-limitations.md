# Ethics and Limitations

Cultural-development research can easily reproduce stereotypes if handled carelessly.

## Limitations

- Synthetic data only.
- No real societies, children, schools, or language communities.
- No validated scale.
- No causal evidence.
- No diagnostic or policy use.
- No ranking of cultures.

## Responsible framing

Culture should be treated as developmental context, meaning, practice, and institution. Cultural difference should not be interpreted as developmental failure. At the same time, cultural analysis should not excuse harm, coercion, exclusion, or deprivation. Responsible work distinguishes cultural difference from developmental threat while keeping attention on power, inequality, migration, institutional recognition, and support.
